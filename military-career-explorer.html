Bonus Strategy

Stack Your Bonuses (Best Combos)
  • Navy SO-ATF: EBSR $15K + EBPST $15K + EBSHP $5K + LRP $65K + EBHS $5K = $105K+
  • Army 18X: EB $42K + Quick Ship $10K + Language $45K + LRP $65K = $162K+
  • Army 19K: $47.5K EB (advertised highest combat arms bonus FY2026)
  • Air Force AFSWC: $40K enlist + $125K retention SRB later = $165K career potential
  • Marines: ONE bonus only — choose highest (QW $15K for +2 yr contract)
When Bonuses Are Paid
  • Navy SO (SEAL): paid AFTER SQT graduation, NOT at boot camp
  • Navy SB (SWCC): paid after Charlie Phase of SWCC training
  • Navy EOD/ND: paid after respective A-school graduation
  • Air Force: 50% at first duty station, remainder spread across contract
  • Army: varies — some at AIT graduation, some spread across enlistment
  • Marines: at School of Infantry or first permanent duty station
Recruiter Leverage Points
  • Quick Ship (ship within 30 days): Army adds $1K–$10K on any eligible MOS
  • Elite PST scores (Navy SO/SB/EOD/ND): $15K additional EBPST bonus
  • College credits: Army +$250–$8K; Navy = E-3 rank advancement at boot camp
  • ASVAB GT 110+: unlocks most Army bonus MOS and IIIA tier amounts
  • Language proficiency: Army 18X Arabic/Korean/Russian = +$30K–$45K
  • HS senior June–Aug ship: Navy adds $3K–$5K EBHS bonus
Real Money Is in Retention
  • Air Force SW SNCOs (PJ/CCT/TACP/SR): $50K–$125K to re-up 3–5 years
  • Navy SEAL E-9 (26–28 yrs): up to $100K retention contract
  • Marines FY2027 SRB: Raiders/Recon/EOD $50K lateral move kicker
  • Army 2026 QTI: performance-based retention, SF/cyber top payers
  • Army cyber (780th MI Bde): up to $81K for 5-year reenlistment
  • Short enlistment SRBs exist for all branches — 3yr contracts available
