Adventure outcomes

Path A: Elite track
Infantry → Recon → possible sniper role → MARSOC attempt. Highest ambition, highest washout and injury risk, highest pressure on family life.
Path B: Broad service track
Infantry → strong fleet Marine → B-billet/MSG/leadership roles → retirement. More stable than the elite pipeline, still meaningful and global.
Path C: Pivot track
Injury, failure, or changed goals lead to another MOS, school progress, and a civilian or hybrid future. Not failure if he pivots with discipline.
